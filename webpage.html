About Me

Born and brought up in the city of Chicago, I have had the most amazing experiences in life. Being and Indian living in America, I have learned to integrate both cultures in the most unique ways. I currently study at Illinois Institute of Technology and I am doing my third year there. I am also a professional Carnatic Musician and I have been giving performances in USA and India for the past 4 years. I run a successful blog and I am a part of Chicago Academy of Indian Fine Arts (a non profit organization interested in promoting Indian fine arts for an affordable price).

Academics

I am pursuing my bachelors in Digital Humanities with a minor in Computer Science. I had a wild ride with academics. I started off at Benedictine University with Pharmacy school. After one year of horrible chemistry, I transferred to IIT and changed my major to Computer Science. Two years of CS and Ireally could not program much anymore. After a lot of research, I decided to switch over to Digital Humanities. I had enough credits to have a minor in computer science.

Music

I have been learning Carnatic Music for the past 14 years. I did my arangetram in 2011 here in Chicago. After that, I started performing in various states here. After starting college, I started performing for the Margazhi Season. Check out my website for more information!

Hobbies

I run a successful blog called Anugraha, where I discuss Indian culture. I try to keep a very open mind about my culture and hence I tend to ask questions that most would avoid. Thses questions have given me the most interesting stories. Apart from blogging, I am a swimmer and a huge Harry Potter fan.
